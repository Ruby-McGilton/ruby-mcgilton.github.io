Characters

This is the provides the information about all significant characters.

If there is a character's profile you would like to add to, or a character you like isn't there, please contact us with the necessary information and we will respond to you as soon as possible.

Nell Jackson

  • Main character of the show
  • Brave, hot-headed, strong desire to make something of herself, very protective of her family
  • Real name is Nelly but hates it and refuses to go by it
  • Widowed but doesn’t seem to care
  • Has powers of super strength and speed given to her by Billy, a fairy only she can see
  • Grew up with her dad and two younger sisters in a pub in Tottenham
  • Played by Louisa Harland

Billy Blind

  • The fairy that gives Nell her powers
  • Kind, supportive, good at giving advice
  • Doesn’t know anything about himself or what he is or why he is supposed to help Nell
  • Played by Nick Mohammed

Roxy Trotter

  • Nell’s teenage sister
  • Very kind, protective, intuitive
  • Has magic powers she hasn’t fully realised yet, but is able to sense when things are wrong
  • In a relationship with Amadin
  • Played by Bo Bragason

George Trotter

  • Nell’s eight year old sister
  • Cheerful, smart, brave
  • Only one in her family who can read
  • Can’t really see without glasses
  • Survived being shot, two surgeries where she was awake/unmedicated, and getting kidnapped and doesn’t seem bothered
  • Fond of stealing
  • Played by Florence Keen

Rasselas/Amadin

  • Kind, considerate, thoughtful
  • Was a slave bought by Tottenham’s magistrate (Lord Blancheford) as a child but ran away to help Nell and her family
  • Name was changed from Amadin to Rasselas when he was a kid without his knowledge, he later found out and changed it back
  • Likes doing silly voices
  • In a relationship with Roxy
  • Played by Enyi Okoronkwo

Charles Devereux

  • Dramatic, kind but usually sides with whoever will help him more, bit of a coward
  • Pretends to be rich but isn’t
  • Becomes a highway robber under the name ‘Isambard Tulley’ to make money
  • Enjoys an adventure
  • Has a fiance but is terrified of her and is only with her for the money
  • Played by Frank Dillane

Thomas Blancheford

  • A villain
  • Son of Tottenham’s magistrate
  • Entitled, spoiled, angry, violent, doesn’t respect women (or anyone really)
  • Murdered his dad, Nell's dad, and also someone's horse
  • Alcoholic
  • Relies on The Earl of Poynton, who is actually manipulating him, for advice
  • Played by Jake Dunn

Sofia Wilmot

  • A villain
  • Daughter of Tottenham’s magistrate and Thomas’ sister
  • Highly intelligent, quiet and reserved, but internally raging at all times
  • Widowed but doesn’t seem to care
  • Learns dark magic from The Earl of Poynton
  • Only had one friend growing up and it was Amadin
  • A good person, just wasn’t hugged enough as a child probably
  • Played by Alice Kremelberg

The Earl of Poynton

  • A villain
  • Real name is Robert Hennessey but just gets called Poynton
  • Power hungry, calculating, intelligent
  • Uses dark magic
  • Loves to gaslight and manipulate
  • Hates the queen and wants to kill her
  • Played by Adrian Lester

Polly Honeycombe

  • Kind, cheerful, hopeless romantic, open-minded
  • From a rich family but isn’t a snob
  • Loves reading and writing
  • Book smart but has no real world intelligence
  • Has a crush on Nell which the other characters find hilarious
  • Played by Ashna Rabheru

Eularia Moggerhanger

  • Intimidating, quick-witted, direct, doesn’t really care about other people
  • In charge of the news sheets
  • Loves gossip and stories she can make money off of, regardless of whether or not they're true
  • Rich
  • Charles’ fiance but she thinks he’s a bit pathetic
  • Played by Joely Richardson
