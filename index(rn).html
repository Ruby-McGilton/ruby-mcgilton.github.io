Welcome!

Welcome to the Renegade Nell website, where we provide information about and discuss all things related to the show!

Renegade Nell is a TV show released in 2024 and is available to watch on Disney Plus. Set in 1705 England, it follows a young woman with magical abilities framed for murder who must turn to highway robbery to survive. While trying to clear her name and protect her family, she discovers she can also use her powers to change the world for good.
